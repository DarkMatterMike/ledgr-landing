❖ Privacy First

No ads. No upsells.
No engagement tricks.

Ledgr is built on one principle: your financial data is yours. We don't sell it, we don't use it to target you, and we don't build features to keep you hooked.

🔒
Read-Only Bank Access

Ledgr connects via Plaid's read-only API. It cannot move money, initiate payments, or touch your accounts in any way.

🔑
No Passwords Stored

Your banking credentials never touch Ledgr's servers. Plaid handles authentication directly with your bank.

🛡️
Encrypted at Rest

All data is encrypted with row-level security. No other user can ever access your account or see your transactions.

🚫
Zero Ads. Forever.

Your data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used to target you. No exceptions and no asterisks.

🤖
Your AI Key

AI features use your own Claude API key. Requests go from your browser directly to Anthropic — Ledgr never proxies your data for AI.

🧘
No Engagement Tricks

No streaks. No dark-pattern notifications. No infinite feed. Open it when you want. Close it when you're done.
